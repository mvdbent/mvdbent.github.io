The truth about MDM Migration: Journey through time as Jamf consulting engineer me and Rob Potvin will talk about the current design of the Apple MDM protocol and what the migration process looks like for moving existing devices from one MDM to another.

This can be fairly difficult, but success is possible with a bit of time, effort and planning. It is not really migrating, but destroying trust.
